OUR STORY

“Our Bakkery was born in the small town of Riviersonderend.

From humble beginnings we have gradually grown and extended the South African farmstall experience around the Western Cape.

We at Bakery pride ourselves on the quality of our product from puff pastry pies to sourdough bread.

A full “sit down” menu with only the freshest product. Free range chicken and eggs, “home” baked breads and lots of love and attention.

Our coffee allegiance lies with Bootlegger Coffee Company. You will struggle to find a better coffee anywhere.

We offer a wide and ever-changing range of retail products from various local suppliers.”
